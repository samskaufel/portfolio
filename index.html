about

I've always enjoyed creating things, whether that be science projects, erector set projects, or snap circuit projects as a kid with my dad, to photography, cooking, fashion, and embroidery as an adult. I pursued a career in healthcare because I enjoyed working with and helping people, but it was missing the creativity aspect that I always get drawn back to.

My background is in exercise physiology where I specialized in coaching, training, and educating clients. I transitioned into web development because I wanted to continue to help make people's lives easier but through a more creative and positive outlet. My experience has brought to my attention the importance of producing a well functioning and well-designed web application.

As a web developer, I appreciate the challenge, but also freedom from building something from scratch. I bring simple and clean designs to functional websites that are intuitive and easy to navigate. I'm looking for a full-time role where I can continue to grow as a developer, where I can bring customer-facing experience to making a product that meets the users needs.
